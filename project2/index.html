Welcome to "Who's That Pokemon?"! You will have a certain number of guesses to figure out the random pokemon. If the pokemon you guess matches the random pokemon in a category, that text will be green. If it does not match, that text will be red. For height and weight, if the random pokemon weighs less or is shorter than your guess, the corresponding category will say "lower". If the random pokemon weighs more or is taller than your guess, the corresponding category will say "higher".
