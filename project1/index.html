ABOUT ME

Hi there! My name is Omar and I'm from Atlanta, GA. I'm a 5th Year Game Design and Development major at the Rochester Institute of Technology. I believe games can be used not only as a medium for entertainment, but also as a way to provide people with experiences they could never have otherwise. That's what I strive to achieve in my games so I can open people up to parts of the world and experiences they never would have dreamed of reaching. When I'm not designing video games, you can find me playing them, watching YouTube, or spending time with friends.
