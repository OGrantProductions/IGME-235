ABOUT ME

Hi there! My name is Omar and I'm from Atlanta, GA. I'm a 3rd Year Game Design and Development major at the Rochester Institute of Technology. I believe games can be used not only as a medium for entertainment, but also as a way to provide people with experiences they could never have otherwise. When I'm not designing video games, you can find me playing them, watching YouTube, or spending time with friends.
